Publications

  • Alimoglu, A., Erdayandi, K., Mustafa, M. A., Cali, Ü. (2025). EDGChain-E: A Decentralized Git-Based Framework for Versioning Encrypted Energy Data. arXiv preprint (accepted - in press).

Research
